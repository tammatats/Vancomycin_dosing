Adult Protocol Helper

Vancomycin Initial + TDM Adjustment

Implements the dosing flow from your diagram: loading/maintenance dose, interval by creatinine clearance, infusion safety limits, and follow-up adjustment using serum vancomycin levels.

Clinical decision support only. Final prescription must be confirmed by physician/pharmacist and local hospital policy.

1) Initial Dosing

Auto CrCl equation: Cockcroft-Gault = ((140 - age) x weight[kg]) / (72 x SCr), and x0.85 for female.

2) Adjust by Serum Vancomycin Level

Reference Rules from Diagram

  • Loading dose: 20-30 mg/kg (actual body weight).
  • Maintenance dose: 15-20 mg/kg per dose.
  • Interval by CrCl: > 50 mL/min = q8-12h, 30-50 = q24h, < 30 = one dose then TDM.
  • Infusion safety: max rate 10 mg/min, max concentration 5 mg/mL.
  • Monitoring reminder in this app: q24h maintenance -> trough 30 min before 3rd dose; CrCl < 30 -> level at 48 hr after loading.
  • Target trough: 10-20 mg/L; MRSA goal AUC/MIC 400-600.
